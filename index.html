Topics to be Covered

  • » Fundamentals of GNSS signal structure, acquisition, tracking anddata processing.
  • » Implementation of GNSS data processing algorithms using MATLAB / Python.
  • » Fundamentals of radar systems, signals and signal processing.
  • » Implementation radar signal processing algorithms on MATLAB /Python.
  • » Building simple radar systems using custom off-the-shelf components.

Who Should Join

  • » B.Tech. and Master students from ECE, aerospace, robotics, control systems and relevant background.
  • » Faculty interested in developing courses on radar, GNSS, sensor fusion, localization and Kalman filtering.
  • » Ph.D. students, industry and research labs who are venturing into radar, GNSS, sensor fusion and estimation theory.
